Timeless is a functional application which lets you analyze your coding activity tracked by WakaTime or wakapi.

Here's some of its functionalities:
- View statistics between different time ranges
- View daily statistics
- View all time statistics
- View your projects and their specific statistics
- View your commits for each project
- Challange other programmers in the leaderboard
And much more to come!
